ABOUT US

Groundwork Solutions is a modern groundwork contractor, specializing in building reliable foundations for small or large-scale projects. We combine proven construction methods with today's technology, using advanced surveying to plan every detail before work begins. This data-informed approach allows our skilled teams to execute excavation, piling, and foundation projects with high precision and efficiency. Our primary goal is to provide a transparent and streamlined process, ensuring your project is built on a dependable foundation, delivered on schedule, and ready for its next phase.

OUR SERVICES

Foundation Works

We manage all foundation needs, from simple residential piling to complex soil reinforcement. Our team designs and delivers the optimal, most secure foundation for your project

Grouting & Injection

Our injection services cost-effectively repair and seal cracks in concrete and rock. This proven method extends a structure's lifespan with minimal environmental disruption

Concrete Works

We specialize in complete concrete solutions up to the finished slab. Our team expertly handles everything from foundations and basements to complex industrial-grade platforms

Groundworks

We manage all phases of ground preparation, from initial excavation and utility installation to final surface grading. Our expertise covers projects of any scale, including public and civil works

Mass Stabilization

We use advanced techniques to solidify soft soils and sediments directly on-site. This creates stable ground, making it ideal for construction in challenging areas like harbors

Soil Remediation

Our in-situ methods safely neutralize contaminated soil directly on-site. This sustainable approach avoids the need for costly and disruptive excavation and transport
