SOLUTIONS

YOUR PROJECT'S TRUSTED FOUNDATION PARTNER

Groundwork Solutions is a modern groundwork contractor, specializing in building reliable foundations from small to large-scale projects. We combine proven construction methods with today's technology, using advanced surveying to plan every detail before work begins. This data-informed approach allows our skilled teams to execute excavation, piling, and foundation projects with high precision and efficiency. Our primary goal is to provide a transparent and streamlined process, ensuring your project is built on a dependable foundation, delivered on schedule, and ready for its next phase.

Our Services

We provide a complete suite of groundwork and civil engineering services, designed to support large-scale construction projects from initial site assessment to final handover. Our expert teams leverage modern technology and proven methods to deliver a safe, efficient, and reliable foundation for your vision.

Utility & Drainage Installation

We expertly install all critical subsurface infrastructure, including storm drainage, water mains, and sewage systems. Our work ensures your site is fully serviced and protected from water-related issues.

Site Preparation & Excavation

The essential first step for any project. We handle everything from land clearing and grading to bulk excavation and rock blasting, using GPS-guided machinery to ensure perfect accuracy from day one.

Piling & Deep Foundations

For large structures or challenging soil conditions, we provide expert piling and deep foundation solutions. Our techniques guarantee long-term stability for the most demanding projects, including data centers and high-rise buildings.

Shoring & Retaining Structures

We design and install robust shoring and retaining walls to ensure site safety and stability. This service is critical for enabling deep excavations, especially on constrained or complex urban sites.

Reinforced Concrete Slabs

Our team specializes in casting high-tolerance, industrial-grade concrete slabs and foundations. We deliver a perfectly finished and durable base structure, engineered to handle heavy loads and meet precise specifications.

Grouting & Injection Services

Using advanced injection techniques, we repair, seal, and strengthen existing concrete structures or ground conditions. This is a cost-effective solution for extending the life of assets and preventing water ingress.

Soil Improvement & Remediation

We transform poor or contaminated ground into a valuable, buildable asset. Our team uses methods like mass stabilization and in-situ remediation to improve soil strength and neutralize pollutants sustainably.

Site Management & Logistics

Beyond the physical work, we offer comprehensive site management. We handle all groundwork logistics, scheduling, and safety protocols, providing you with a single, reliable point of contact for a seamless operation.
